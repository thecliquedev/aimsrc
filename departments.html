The Akash Institute of Medical Science and Research Centre is a centre of excellence for learning, teaching and patient care. Whether it is the undergraduate MBBS or postgraduate programmes or research in basic or clinical sciences, our faculty aims to provide an environment where students and researchers are empowered to seek solutions to today's health care challenges. The undergraduate MBBS and postgraduate courses follow the RGUHS and NMC guidelines. Our foundations for great medical education are rooted in basic science, clinical experience and in-depth research, and deep curriculum implementation.

The student doctors get to study under the guidance of expert faculty and world-renowned speakers. Experts from the industry share their knowledge on how to equip their students with necessary skills for a successful career in medicine. The research cell is responsible for various initiatives and partnerships to support budding clinicians and researchers

UNDERGRADUATE

The professors deploy a variety of innovative interactive learning methods such as e-learning modules, student assessments, and standardized patients and simulators, through the Medical Education Unit. Traditional learning methods such as lectures in the classroom, practical lessons, seminars, and small group activities continue to be significant in student learning. Faculty are nationally recognized for teaching and research in medicine. Students have many opportunities to engage with medical professionals through community clinics, practice settings, simulation teaching laboratories, and enrichment activities.

POSTGRADUATE

The application of learning components of hospital safety, waste management, and safe medication communication are constantly applied. Postgraduates receive specialized instruction in research methodology and statistical analysis, which will benefit them in their future professions.

The Skill labs provide training in the form of simulations and tissue dissections, emphasizing their scientific foundation.

We are dedicated to the education of excellent physicians and specialists, and our institution places a strong emphasis on the fundamental scientific disciplines as well as ethical and professional considerations that are required to attain this goal.
