Fresher's Day Report - “ADVAYA” 2016-17

Fresher’s day for the Ist batch students admitted during 2016-17 was organized on 30th December 2016. The function was presided over by the Chairperson of Akash Education & Development Trust, Smt. Pushpa Muniraju. Other dignitaries on the Dias were Founder Secretary of the trust Shri. K Muniraju, Vice chairman of the trust Sri. Amar Muniraju, Director & Managing trustee Dr. H V Satish Babu, Mch (Neurosurgery), Principal Dr. DS Vasudeva MD (Pathology) and Medical Superintendent of the affiliated teaching hospital Dr Govindaraju V, MD (Biochemistry). The Principal, Dr. D.S. Vasudeva delivered the welcome note to the gathering & unveiled the name of “Advaya” for the Ist batch of students admitted during 2016-17. The Director, HV Satish Babu gave an in depth narration of the milestones of Akash Institute of Medical Sciences and Research Centre and its affiliated teaching hospital. He expressed his heartfelt thanks to each of the staff members across all cadres and class, who contributed for the development of the institution. The Vice chairman spoke on the occasion highlighting the dream of developing this institution into a premier institute. During the function, Dr Shailendranath MS (HOD of Anatomy), Dr Dayananda G (HOD of Physiology) and Dr Wilma D Silvia CR (HOD of Biochemistry) were honored for their contribution towards the development of the institution. Dr Shantharam V (Warden, Boys hostel & Associate Professor of Anatomy) Dr Sudha V Patil (Warden, Girls Hostel & Associate Professor of OBG) were also honored for their contribution. The inaugural function was concluded with vote of thanks by Dr Govindaraju V Medical Superintendent, Akash Teaching Hospital. Following the Inaugural Program, there was a great treat of cultural recipe by the Ist batch students of AIMS & RC. It included a series of events ranging from individual to group performances. There were solo and group songs, solo and group dances. The songs were a real melody to the ears and the dance performances a visual extravaganza. Adding a tinge of perfection were the stage, LED back drop and other arrangements for which the management is hugely credited. The performances which included students in different ethnic and linguistic groups were perfectly compered by a group of very talented students. It was a showcase of talent, participation, enthusiasm, performance so well-orchestrated to bring out the much awaited joy & smile in the student group. The staff & Parents of students were equally delighted by the show of talent & the arrangements. They were all in praise of the organizers, the Director & the Principal in particular. The discipline demonstrated with no room for misconduct was widely appreciated. Towards the end of the program the student community was left baffled when the staff decided to shake a leg – Ramp walk. It was an excellent presentation with a very short but a good preparation. The program indeed was truly an “ADVAYA” – One of its kind & next to none.
