Pharmacovigilance Committee

The college is recognized as Adverse Drug Reaction Monitoring Centre (AMC) by Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of India under Pharmacovigilance Program of India (PvPI) on 16 June, 2017.

Surveillance and reporting of adverse drug reactions to PvPI, National Coordination Centre, Indian Pharmacopoiea Commission is performed regularly. Hemovigilance programme is also active and the adverse reactions are reported on regular basis.

The Committee meets quarterly to highlight the importance of ADR monitoring. The statistics of the reported adverse drug reactions are presented. This meeting is also the forum where various drug related reactions are discussed in detail.
